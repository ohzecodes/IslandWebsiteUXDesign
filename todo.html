Activities in Taniti

water snorkelling

Cost free or $50/hour

Snorkeling is a great way to get the best view of the ocean floor. Itâ€™s relaxing and fun. If you have never done it before we would strongly advise that you snorkel with a professional guide.

Forrest ziplining

Cost $20/Person

An outdoor adventure at the rainforest. Climb high into the treetops and tackle an adrenaline-fuelled course of 16 ziplines. The length of the zipline course ranges from 0.5 miles to 2 miles.

enjoying at the beach

Cost Free

The sandy and rocky beaches of Yellow Leaf Bay, and the surrounding white sand are perfect for beach goers to relax. The water is crystal clear, providing a perfect reflection of the surrounding natural surroundings.

visit an active volcano

Cost Free

Volcanoes are beautiful and scary at the same time. They have many attractions apart from the actual danger in their vicinity. Hot springs and geysers are among other attractions that keep the tourist coming back again and again.

go fishing!

Cost $20

Going on a fishing tour is one of the most popular activities in Taniti. This activity is available all year round. The locations where you can go fishing are: Liman Bay, inlets and bays along the coastline of Taniti and Buluku Islands, Limu Dance Fair

Helicopter Tour

Cost $50

On a helicopter tour, you get to experience the island from a bird eye view. This allows you to see more of the breathtaking natural beauty that makes this place so special.

food in Taniti

Taniti currently has 10 restaurants: five serve mostly local fish and rice, three serve American-style meals, and two serve Pan-Asian cuisine.

Taniti.gov

Taniti is a small, tropical island in the Pacific. The island has an area of less than 500 square miles, the terrain is varied and includes both sandy and rocky beaches, a small but safe harbor, lush tropical rainforests, and a mountainous interior that includes a small, active volcano.
